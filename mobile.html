About Wild Landscapes
Our service includes free consultation and quotation from the smaller requirements of laying turf to a complete overhaul and redesign of a garden. We have over 12 years experience delivering high quality projects within the industry covering hard and soft landscaping

Design
We are happy to discuss your ideas and requirements to produce a scale drawing and planting scheme
Fencing
We supply and erect all types of domestic fencing and other wooden garden products including sheds, pergolas and decking
Turfing
Nothing finishes a garden off better than a well laid lawn. We clear the area, cultivate and level to prepare the ground before laying the turf
Brickwork
From small seating walls to raised planters to edging your lawn or flower bed we can use bricks to great effect
Planting
The finishing touch to the garden is important to get right, from choosing shape and colour to correctly preparing the soil
Water Features
The sound of water is guaranteed to bring tranquillity to any garden. We can offer construction of small ornamental ponds, wildlife friendly bog gardens or all types of fountains
Paving
We can lay a range of materials from Sandstone, brick, cobbles and concrete flag and block
Maintenance
We offer grass cutting, treework, hedge cutting, weeding and clearance work for anything from small back gardens to large estates
Commercial
Our commercial landscape services include ground preparation, planting of trees and shrubs, seeding, turfing and maintenance. We are fully qualified with CSCS landscape ticket, CPCS tickets for 360 excavator, forward tipping dumper and signaller/slinger, NPTC in chainsaw and pesticide application.
